Research

User research: summary

I conducted a research on the users to understand the stress users using food apps experience when ordering food and also what emotions do these users get when ordering food? At the end of the research, I was able to identify some paint point like stress encounter when preparing meal and their time constraint making it hard to create time for cooking. These information gotten from these user research help me better understand the users and how to create a product that help with their need.


User research

Pain points

Stress of cooking

Some users see cooking as a work and will be glad to have a product that eliminate such work but wouldn't stop them from having their favourite meal

Time contraint

Another challeges faced by som users is not having enough time to prepare their meal due to their busy work life. One of the ways of catering for the need of these users is by adding a reminder and or schedule feature to the app.

Ease of Use

Ability of users to easily use the food app efficiently. One of the way of overcoing this pain is by providing text, voice and image search feature on the food app.

Ideation

Low-fidelity prototype

To create a low-fidelity prototype, I connected all of the screens involved in the primary user flow of ordering a food.At this point, I received feedback as insights from my usability study on my designs about things like the placement of buttons and page organization. I made sure to listen to their feedback, and I implemented several suggestions in places that addressed user pain points.



Usability Study Findings

I am creating a new app to help people order food online. I need to find out if the main user experience, in ordering food online, is easy for users to complete. After the research, I was able to discover that some of the challenges faced by the users are:

An estimated time of when food will be ready

Inability to easily find food items

Uncertainty of the quality of food being produced by a restaurant.

Easy access to local food/ meal




Design

Mockups

My goal is to build an easily accessible food app. As a way of archiving this goal, the insights obtained from the observation and findings led to the addition of a picture search feature on the app by adding the camera icon.


Another goal is to make it easy for users to know the quality of the food produced by a food vendor/ restaurant on the food app, this is achieved by adding a rating feature on the app.
